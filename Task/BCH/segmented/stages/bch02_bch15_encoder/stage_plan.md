# BCH-02 BCH(15,11,1) encoder

Implements only the 11-bit to 15-bit systematic encoder under the BCH-01 convention. Decoder, syndrome table, frame segmentation, AWGN, whole-block BCH, BM, Chien, and GF generalization are out of scope. Gate requires all 2048 messages, zero remainder, systematic preservation, uniqueness, independent reference agreement, and no Common or out-of-scope change.
